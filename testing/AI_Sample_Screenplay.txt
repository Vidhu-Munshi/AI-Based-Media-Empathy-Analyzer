INT. CITY PARK - SUNSET

A QUIET PARK FILLED WITH PEOPLE WALKING, CHILDREN PLAYING, AND BIRDS
SINGING. THE SUN IS SETTING BEHIND THE TREES.

AARON SITS ALONE ON A BENCH, STARING AT THE GROUND. HIS FRIEND EMMA
NOTICES HIM AND WALKS OVER.

                        EMMA
            MIND IF I SIT HERE?

                        AARON
            GO AHEAD.

EMMA SITS BESIDE HIM. THERE IS A MOMENT OF SILENCE.

                        EMMA
            YOU DON'T LOOK LIKE YOURSELF TODAY.

                        AARON
            SOMETIMES IT FEELS LIKE NO MATTER
            HOW HARD I TRY, I'M NEVER GOOD
            ENOUGH.

                        EMMA
            WHO TOLD YOU THAT?

                        AARON
            NOBODY HAD TO. I KEEP TELLING
            MYSELF.

EMMA SMILES GENTLY.

                        EMMA
            THEN MAYBE IT'S TIME YOU STOPPED
            LISTENING TO THAT VOICE.

AARON LOOKS AT HER.

                        AARON
            THAT'S EASIER SAID THAN DONE.

                        EMMA
            TRUE. BUT EVERY SMALL STEP COUNTS.
            YOU DON'T HAVE TO FIX YOUR WHOLE
            LIFE TODAY.

A YOUNG BOY TRIPS WHILE RUNNING. HIS FATHER HELPS HIM STAND.

                        FATHER
            FALLING IS PART OF LEARNING.
            COME ON, LET'S TRY AGAIN.

AARON WATCHES THEM.

                        AARON
            I WISH I COULD THINK LIKE THAT.

                        EMMA
            YOU CAN. IT JUST TAKES PRACTICE.
            KINDNESS IS A SKILL, ESPECIALLY
            WHEN YOU'RE TALKING TO YOURSELF.

AARON TAKES A DEEP BREATH.

                        AARON
            MAYBE TOMORROW WILL BE BETTER.

                        EMMA
            DON'T WAIT FOR TOMORROW.
            START WITH THE NEXT FIVE MINUTES.

AARON LAUGHS FOR THE FIRST TIME.

                        AARON
            THANK YOU.

                        EMMA
            THAT'S WHAT FRIENDS ARE FOR.

THEY STAND AND BEGIN WALKING THROUGH THE PARK AS THE SUN SETS.

                                                     FADE OUT.
