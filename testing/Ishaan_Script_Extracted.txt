Ishaan is intensely drumming in the recreation room in
the

Kya mein andar aa sakta hu?

Ishaan keeps drumming and acquiesces with his head.
Aditya picks up his guitar and starts strumming it.
Ishaan takes a pause in drumming.

ki aaplications bhej di?

Ishaan drums a light beat.
